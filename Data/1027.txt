Melissa Zwanger and Evan Philip Oster, candidates for dual M.B.A. and M.A. degrees at the Wharton School and the Lauder Institute of Management and International Studies of the University of Pennsylvania, were married last night in a penthouse at the New York Hilton. Rabbi Leonard Stern, assisted by Cantor Nancy Ginsburg, performed the ceremony. The bride, a daughter of Dr. and Mrs. Jerome Zwanger of Massapequa Park, L.I., graduated from Harvard College and was formerly an international-marketing executive for Estee Lauder in New York. Her father is president of the New York chapter of the American College of Radiology and co-director of radiology at Central General Hospital in Plainview, L.I. Her mother, Bernice Lomazov Zwanger, is a lawyer. Mr. Oster, the son of Mr. and Mrs. Leonard Oster of Long Beach, L.I., graduated from Brown University and is a former consultant for Organization Resources Counselors in New York. His father is secretary of Parks-Seidman, a construction company in Bellerose, L.I. His mother, Jacqueline Oster, is a retired district director of art education for the City of New York. Betsy Zwanger was maid of honor for her sister and James Robert Haddleston was the best man.
