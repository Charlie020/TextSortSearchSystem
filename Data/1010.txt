Dr. and Mrs. Walter W. Tuchman of New York have announced the engagement of their daughter, Laura Hicks Tuchman, to Adrian Philip Crawley, the son of Mr. and Mrs. Peter Joseph Crawley of Tenterden, South Kent, England. Miss Tuchman graduated magna cum laude from Swarthmore College, at which she was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. She is a doctoral candidate in English at the University of Toronto, at which her fiance is a doctoral candidate in medical biophysics. Her father is a clinical associate professor of psychiatry at the New York Medical College. Mr. Crawley is a graduate of Clare College of Cambridge University. His father retired as the assistant chief architect of the Borough Council in London.
