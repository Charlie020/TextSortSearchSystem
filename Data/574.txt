At the West End Collegiate Church in Manhattan yesterday Diane Teresa Moore, the daughter of Ann D. Moore of Jackson Heights, Queens, and the late Charles J. Moore, was married to James R. Weldon, the son of Mr. and Mrs. Henry R. Weldon of New York. The ceremony was performed by the Rev. Kenneth A. Gorsuch, pastor of the church, and the Rev. Lee Smith, a Roman Catholic priest. Mrs. Weldon is an administrative assistant in the client service department at the Morgan Guaranty Trust Company of New York, of which her father was a vice president. She attended St. John's University. Mr. Weldon is the marketing, planning and evaluation manager for the General Foods Corporation in White Plains and an adjunct instructor of marketing at the Graduate School of Business of Fordham University. He graduated from Fordham and received an M.B.A. degree from the University of Chicago. His father, who is retired, was the founder and president of Weldon Farm Products in New York.
