Eckert of Palmerton, Pa., and the late Nancy Butz Eckert, was married to Andrew Lane Shea, a son of Nancy Bowditch of New York and Peter L. Shea of Locust Valley, L.I. The Rev. James Laubach performed the ceremony. Mrs. Shea recently graduated from the financial management program of the General Electric Company in Schenectady, N.Y. She is an alumna of Lehigh University. Her father is the president of the Butz Lumber Company in Palmerton. Mr. Shea is a cogeneration project developer for Inter-Power of New York, a consulting firm in Albany. He is an alumnus of Stanford University, from which he also received a master's degree in civil engineering. His father is a managing director of Hydrocarbon Energy Inc., an oil and gas investment company in New York.
