Guthrie Speers Jr. Dorothy Harris Taggart, a senior vice president of Shearson Asset Management Inc., and James E. Osborn, an executive vice president of the Edgewood Management Company, a financial consulting and management concern, were married yesterday at the Presbyterian Church of New Canaan. The Rev. T. Guthrie Speers Jr. performed the ceremony. The bride, a graduate of Wellesley College and Teachers College of Columbia University, is the daughter of Mrs. Lester L. Harris of Madison, Wis., and the late Mr. Harris. Mr. Osborn, a graduate of Yale University, is the son of the late Mr. and Mrs. Richard Osborn of Fall River, Mass. The bride's previous marriage ended in divorce, and the bridegroom was widowed.
