If the institution's main thrust is to win, fill the stadium and keep the alumni happy, then let the freshman play and the athlete be damned. The real question is: What is the purpose of college athletics? If the institution's main thrust is to win, fill the stadium and keep the alumni happy, then let the freshman play and the athlete be damned. If any educational institution would ask itself how the athlete is benefiting from playing varsity athletics, it would have a very tough time answering in front of the mirror. One resounding vote against, from a former Division I basketball player.  ERNEST BRAVERMAN Neponsit, N.Y. QUESTION OF THE WEEK
