Announcement has been made by Mr. and Mrs. Paul Weinberg of Merrick, L.I., of the engagement of their daughter Elyce Weinberg to Mitch Neuhauser, a son of Arthur Neuhauser of East Brunswick, N.J., and the late Roslyn Neuhauser. A spring wedding is planned. The future bride graduated from Adelphi University and is an editorial coordinator at Vogue Patterns magazine in New York. Her father is a general contractor. Mr. Neuhauser, a public-relations consultant, graduated from the University of Wisconsin at Madison. His father, who is retired, was the head of the cultural news copy desk at The New York Times.
