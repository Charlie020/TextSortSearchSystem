A March wedding is planned. Mr. and Mrs. Robert A. Mendelsohn of White Plains have announced the engagement of their daughter, Linda L. Mendelsohn, to Frank A. Rosenbaum, the son of Mr. and Mrs. Norris Rosenbaum of Boca Raton, Fla. A March wedding is planned. Miss Mendelsohn, the assistant director of taxes for Warner Communications in New York, graduated from Cornell University and received an M.B.A. degree from Northwestern University. Her father is a lawyer in White Plains. Her mother, Leah L. Mendelsohn, teaches the fifth grade in the White Plains school system. Mr. Rosenbaum, the parts manager for the All Makes Heating and Air Conditioning Corporation in Eastchester, N.Y., received an associate degree in fire-science technology and an associate degree in accounting from Westchester Community College. His father, who is retired, was a custom designer and sales representative in New York for the I. Schwartz Glass and Mirror Company. His mother, Elinor Rosenbaum, who is retired, was a remedial-reading teacher for the New York school system.
