Marvin Bailey scored 15 points and pulled down 8 rebounds today to lead Canisius to a 71-46 basketball victory over Colgate. Canisius forced 33 Colgate turnovers and made 19 steals, a school record, as the Golden Griffins broke a three-game losing streak and Colgate suffered its 28th straight loss.
