Susan Louise Quittmeyer, a mezzo-soprano with the San Francisco Opera since 1979, and James Peppler Morris, a leading bass-baritone with the Metropolitan Opera since 1971, were married yesterday in Port Washington, L.I. The Rev. Kurt Von Roeschlaub performed the ceremony at St. Joseph's Episcopal Church. The bride, a magna cum laude graduate of Illinois Wesleyan University who attended the Manhattan School of Music, is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Robert T. Quittmeyer of Port Washington. Her father, an executive-in-residence and a visiting pro fessor at the Columbia Graduate School of Business, is the former chairman of the Amstar Corporation, a diversified holding company. Mr. Morris, the son of Geraldine Morris of Towson, Md., and the late James D. Morris, attended the University of Maryland, the Peabody Conservatory of Music in Baltimore and the Academy of Vocal Arts in Philadelphia. His father was the vice president and general sales manager of the Koontz Creamery in Baltimore. His previous marriage ended in divorce, as did that of his bride.
