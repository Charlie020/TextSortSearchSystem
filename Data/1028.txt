At the First Church of Christ, Congregational, in Farmington, Conn., yesterday Claire Asenath Zimmerman was married to Christopher John Ratte by the Rev. Ned W. Edwards Jr. The bride is the daughter of Dr. Rachel Phillips Belash of Farmington, Conn., who is head of Miss Porter's School, and Franklin B. Zimmerman of Philadelphia, a professor of music at the University of Pennsylvania. Mr. Ratte is the son of Dr. Lou Ratte and Dr. John Ratte of Winsdor, Conn. His father is the headmaster of the Loomis Chaffee School, and his mother is a visiting scholar in the history of India at Smith College. Mrs. Ratte, who graduated from the Milton Academy and the University of Pennsylvania, is a candidate for a Master of Architecture degree at the Harvard University Graduate School of Design. Her husband, a doctoral candidate in archeology at the University of California at Berkeley, graduated from Phillips Academy in Andover, Mass., and Harvard College.
