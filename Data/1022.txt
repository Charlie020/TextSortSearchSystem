Laura Marie Givan, a daughter of Dr. and Mrs. Thurman Boyd Givan Jr. of Cold Spring Harbor, L.I., was married there yesterday to Dennis T. Wilson, a son of Mrs. John Wilson of Amityville, L.I., and the late Mr. Wilson. The Rev. T. Carleton Lee performed the ceremony at St. John's Episcopal Church. Dale Anne Givan was her sister's maid of honor. John M. Wilson was best man for his brother. The bride, who will keep her name professionally, is the designer-coat buyer for Macy's New York. She graduated from Albright College. Her father, a pediatrician, is a partner in the North Shore Medical Group in Huntington, L.I. Mrs. Wilson is a granddaughter of Dorothy Givan of Moorestown, N.J., and the late Dr. Givan of Brooklyn and Hilton Head Island, S.C., a pediatrician and a president of the New York State Medical Society. Mr. Wilson is a vice president of the Turner Power Group, a developer of cogeneration plants in New York. He is an alumnus of the State University of New York at Albany and Brooklyn Law School. His previous marriage ended in divorce. His father was a vice president of Hartz Mountain Industries in Secaucus, N.J.
