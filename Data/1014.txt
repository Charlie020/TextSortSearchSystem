Announcement has been made by Mr. and Mrs. Frank A. Sprole of Vero Beach, Fla., and Darien, Conn., of the engagement of their daughter Sarah Elizabeth Sprole to Richard Obregon, the son of Dr. and Mrs. Gonzalo Obregon of Sacramento, Calif. The future bride, who graduated from the Hotchkiss School and with the class of '82 from Stanford University, is a travel associate at the Stanford alumni office. Her father, now retired, was vice chairman of the Bristol-Myers Company. Mr. Obregon, also a 1982 graduate of Stanford, is a fourth-year student at the Medical School of the University of California at San Francisco. His father is a surgeon practicing in Sacramento.
