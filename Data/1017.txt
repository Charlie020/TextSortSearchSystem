Her fiance is a son of Mr. and Mrs. John Garry Valk of Sea Island, Ga., and Cutchogue, L.I. A May wedding is planned. The engagement of Barbara Burke to J. Lansing Valk has been announced by Mr. and Mrs. Robert A. Burke of New Canaan, Conn., and Quogue, L.I., parents of the future bride. Her fiance is a son of Mr. and Mrs. John Garry Valk of Sea Island, Ga., and Cutchogue, L.I. A May wedding is planned. Miss Burke, a graduate of the Ethel Walker School and magna cum laude of Duke University, is director of blood services for the Norwalk-Wilton Chapter of the American Red Cross in Norwalk, Conn. Her father, who is retired, was the founder and president of the Chemetal Corporation in South Norwalk, Conn. The future bridegroom, a graduate of Colgate University and the Albany Law School of Union University, received a M.B.A. degree from Fordham University. He is an investment broker at the Wilton, Conn., office of A. G. Edwards Inc. His father, now retired, had been a vice president of Time Inc. and publisher of Sports Illustrated and Life magazines.
