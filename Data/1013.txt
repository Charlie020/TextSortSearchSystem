Ines Maria Garcia, the daughter of Irene Perez de Garcia of San Juan, P.R., and the late Jose Ramon Garcia, was married at St. John's Episcopal Cathedral in San Juan yesterday to John Stevens Keim, the son of Mr. and Mrs. John Oram Keim Jr. of Millington, N.J. Bishop Francisco Reus-Froylan performed the ceremony. The bride, a graduate of Barnard College, is the manager of the Pottery Barn in Scarsdale, N.Y. Mr. Keim, an alumnus of Hamilton College, is the research supervisor for Interior Design magazine in New York.
