285|1987\01\02|2 U.S. Moslems to Try Again To Gain Release of Hostages|Two Moslem Americans said yesterday in New York that they would renew their quest for the release of American hostages in Lebanon.|Two Moslem Americans said yesterday in New York that they would renew their quest for the release of American hostages in Lebanon. Two Moslem Americans said yesterday in New York that they would renew their quest for the release of American hostages in Lebanon. The two men, Dr. M. T. Mehdi and Dale Shaheen of the National Council on Islamic Affairs, will go to Beirut in mid-January in an attempt to make contact with the captors. They sought the release of the hostages last month, but failed to make useful contacts.
