Wendy Elizabeth Buck and John Kennedy Brown were married yesterday at St. James' Episcopal Church in New York by Bishop Robert R. Spears Jr. The bride is a daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Charles Austin Buck of Bernardsville, N.J. Her husband is a son of Mr. and Mrs. J. Crosby Brown of Blue Hill, Me. Mrs. Brown was attended by her sister, Belinda Buck Kielland, as matron of honor. J. Crosby Brown 4th was best man for his brother. The bride until recently was an assistant vice president of the Chancellor Corporation, a Boston equipment-leasing concern. She graduated from the Ethel Walker School and Connecticut College. Her father is chairman of Leonard J. Buck & Company in Morristown, N.J., a transportation and industrial chemical sales concern. Mr. Brown, a Thomas Jefferson Fellow in the American history doctoral program at the University of Virginia, graduated from the Hotchkiss School and Emory University. His father is a retired vice president of the First Pennsylvania Bank in Philadelphia.
